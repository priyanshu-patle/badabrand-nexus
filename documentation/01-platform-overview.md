# Platform Overview

This platform is a full-stack IT services and digital marketplace system for `Badabrand Technologies`.

## Main Areas

- Public website with corporate pages, services, pricing, marketplace, contact, and legal pages
- Customer portal with orders, invoices, payments, project tracker, proposals, contracts, files, and support
- Admin panel with CMS, services, products, billing, users, support, marketing, and project controls
- Developer area for assigned work, tickets, and delivery tracking

## Core Business Modules

- Services catalog
- Pricing plans
- Digital marketplace products
- Orders and receipts
- GST-ready invoices
- Manual, bank, and QR payment review
- Support ticketing
- Notifications
- Proposal generation
- Contract signing
- File sharing
- Referral and coupon system
