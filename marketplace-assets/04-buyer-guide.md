# Buyer Guide

This guide explains the first steps after purchase.

## First Login

1. Login as admin.
2. Open `System Settings`.
3. Add support email, WhatsApp, SEO defaults, and social links.
4. Open `CMS & Branding` and set logo, favicon, hero content, and footer text.
5. Open `Content Hub` and update legal pages, blog, careers, FAQ, portfolio, and team records.

## Store Setup

1. Create services and pricing plans.
2. Add marketplace products such as themes, plugins, software, or templates.
3. Review invoices, payment statuses, support tickets, and order workflows.
