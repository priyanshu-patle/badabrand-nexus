# License And Usage

Suggested package license notes for marketplace delivery.

- License type: `Single end-product commercial license`
- Resale rights: `Not included unless separately negotiated`
- Source editing: `Allowed for buyer-owned deployments`
- Redistribution: `Not allowed as a competing standalone product without permission`
- Support scope: `Installation help, bug fixes, and minor guidance during the support window`

Suggested buyer rules:

1. One purchase covers one production business or end product.
2. The buyer may customize design, branding, and content for their own business or client project.
3. The buyer may not resell the codebase as an unchanged standalone script marketplace item.
4. Major custom integrations, gateway work, or framework migration are outside standard support.
